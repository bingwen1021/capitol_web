Capitol (Hebei) Precision Engineering Co., Ltd. is a Sino-foreign joint venture established by Capitol (Hebei) Machinery Co., Ltd. and Singapore CAPITOL GRAVURE INDUSTRIES PTE.LTD. We integrate global top-tier processing equipment and engineering technology to deliver high-value solutions for surface engineering and precision manufacturing.

凯必盛（河北）专用设备有限公司是一家由凯必拓（河北）机械有限公司与新加坡CAPITOL GRAVURE INDUSTRIES PTE.LTD.共同投资设立的中外合资企业。我们融合全球顶尖工艺装备与工程技术，致力于为客户提供高价值的表面工程与精密制造解决方案。

High-End Industrial Roll Precision Manufacturing:
Focusing on R&D of precision ceramic anilox rolls. Relying on precision CNC centers and ALE (UK) / DV (Italy) laser engraving machines to ensure mesh uniformity.

高端工业辊类精密制造：
专注于各类工业辊及精密陶瓷网纹辊的研发与制造。依托精密CNC加工中心及英国ALE、意大利DV激光雕刻机，确保辊体精度与网纹均匀性。

High-Performance Thermal Spraying:
Using Praxair Plasma and UniqueCoat HVOF systems. Coatings feature bond strength ≥70MPa and porosity ≤0.5%, significantly enhancing wear resistance, corrosion resistance, and high-temperature performance for critical components. Our services are widely applied in aerospace, energy, petrochemical, and heavy machinery sectors.

高性能热喷涂表面工程服务：
采用美国普莱克斯等离子及UniqueCoat超音速火焰喷涂系统。涂层具备结合强度≥70MPa、孔隙率≤0.5%等优异特性，能显著提升关键部件的耐磨、耐蚀与抗高温性能，广泛应用于航空航天、能源、石油化工及重型机械等领域。
